# No-Text Background Prompts

These prompts create only the visual background contract. Replace every `{{variable}}`. Do not ask the image model to render dates, prices, product names, Chinese text, English labels, Logos, watermarks, buttons, or interface copy.

Use this shared negative instruction for every template:

> No letters, words, numerals, Logos, watermarks, price figures, fake labels, fake UI, or illegible typography. Keep the specified text fields visually quiet and empty for deterministic typesetting.

## T01 · Campaign hero

Create a `{{ratio}}` campaign-poster background for `{{theme}}`. Use `{{aesthetic_profile}}` with `{{main_color_strategy}}`. Build one dominant visual event around `{{person_or_product}}`, occupying the `{{subject_region}}`. Reserve a quiet title field in `{{title_region}}` and a separate high-contrast date/benefit field in `{{critical_region}}`. Use `{{material_language}}` sparingly. Keep the subject recognizable, mobile-first, and professionally art-directed.

Typesetting specification: exact campaign title, date/time, one primary benefit, brand mark, optional qualifier and CTA.

## T02 · Weekly schedule

Create a `{{ratio}}` schedule background with `{{date_count}}` clearly separated date regions and one priority-session region. Use `{{aesthetic_profile}}`, a stable underlying grid, consistent card geometry, and one highlight accent. Reserve all date, time, and session areas as clean empty fields. Add `{{optional_hero_subject}}` only if it does not compete with schedule readability.

Typesetting specification: schedule title, dates, times, session names, today/priority marker and platform note.

## T03 · Time capsule

Create a transparent wide capsule component with visually distinct empty fields for date, live-state marker, time, and short CTA. Use `{{main_color}}` and `{{contrast_color}}`, consistent radii, and generous transparent exterior padding. Do not render symbols or text.

Typesetting specification: exact date, time, live marker and CTA of no more than 12 Chinese characters.

## T04 · Single-product story

Create a `{{ratio}}` single-product background featuring `{{product_or_person}}` at roughly 55-70 percent of the composition. Use `{{aesthetic_profile}}`, `{{material_language}}`, and coherent lighting. Reserve one product-name field, up to three short selling-point fields, and an optional price/benefit field. Include up to `{{detail_image_count}}` framed detail regions with consistent treatment.

Typesetting specification: exact brand/product identity, zero-to-three supplied selling points, exact price/benefit mechanism and qualifier.

## T05 · Multi-product catalog

Create a `{{ratio}}` modular catalog background for `{{product_count}}` products using `{{one_column_or_two_by_two}}`. Use `{{aesthetic_profile}}`, equal module geometry, consistent image fields, shared baselines, and one restrained accent system. Keep every product area recognizable and reserve clean fields for identity, short selling points, and optional benefit badges.

Typesetting specification: category title and exact per-product identity, up to two supplied selling points, price/benefit and qualifier.

## T06 · Thematic long-form

Create a chaptered long-form visual system for `{{theme}}` using `{{subject_specific_material_or_pattern}}`. Build a strong opener followed by `{{chapter_count}}` repeatable modules with controlled rhythm changes every two to three chapters. Reserve empty fields for chapter titles, real metadata, body copy, and closing date/CTA. Let the cultural material frame the content rather than cover it.

Typesetting specification: theme title, hook, ordered chapter titles and body, real names/locations, closing date/time, CTA and source qualifier.

## T07 · Interaction or benefit

Create a `{{ratio}}` interaction background for `{{interaction_type}}`. Make the question/puzzle/benefit region the dominant event. Reserve a separate clean field for no more than four numbered rules, plus participation, announcement, and compliance fields. Use `{{aesthetic_profile}}` and one high-contrast benefit accent without filling every gap with promotional decoration.

Typesetting specification: exact title, question or benefit, numbered rules, participation method, announcement time and disclaimer. Keep the answer off the question image when required.
